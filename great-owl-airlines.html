Launching a New Airline from Jaipur

A Data-Driven Market, Forecasting & Network Strategy Project

Home Projects

1. Overview

India’s aviation sector is expanding rapidly, yet Jaipur remains underserved internationally. This project evaluates whether a new airline — Great Owl Airlines — could successfully operate from Jaipur between 2026–2027.

2. Market Understanding

I analysed Jaipur’s catchment area, mapped passenger flows, and assessed competition from Delhi. Despite strong demand drivers, Jaipur lacks sufficient international connectivity.

3. Forecasting Demand — Gravity + Time-Series

Gravity Model demand estimate: 10,434 – 72,237 annual passengers

Time-Series Forecast: New route introduction improves total traffic stability and trend strength.

4. Airline Simulation Results

Using Airline Online, Great Owl Airlines grew to 13 international destinations. Asian routes performed strongly, while London underperformed.

5. Financial Performance

  • 2026 Profit: $10.9M
  • 2027 Loss: $16M (maintenance + advertising overspend)

6. Final Recommendation

The airline is viable with conditions: right-size aircraft, prioritise strong Asian markets, improve maintenance scheduling, and tighten cost control.
