Airline Economics

Market Structure Evaluation of Europe–North Atlantic Routes (2008–2023)

Home Projects

1. Overview

The Europe–North Atlantic corridor is one of the most strategically important long-haul aviation markets in the world. This study evaluates changes in competition and market structure between 2008 and 2023.

2. Objective

To analyse whether the EU–NA market became more or less concentrated over time, using airline market shares, alliance dominance, and HHI.

3. Methodology

  • Market share analysis
  • Herfindahl–Hirschman Index (HHI)
  • Alliance vs independent carrier comparison
  • COVID-19 distortion analysis

4. Market Structure Findings

The analysis shows that alliances maintained strong dominance, with HHI indicating a moderately concentrated oligopoly.

5. COVID-19 Impact

HHI spiked sharply during the pandemic, reflecting temporary dominance by a few carriers due to market contraction.

6. Conclusion

The EU–NA market is moderately concentrated, recovering competitiveness after COVID while remaining structurally alliance-driven.
